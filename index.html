Biography

I started my professional career by teaching English in Japanese public schools for four years. I am a current student of the full-time Immersive Engineering Bootcamp at Thinkful. Like many people, I came out of college with no idea what sort of career I wanted nor my motivation.

What draws me to programming is the creation aspect. Starting from nothing, planning out your project, layering on each component and then seeing your hard work congeal into a tangible object. As a tone-deaf creative individual that can't draw, this motivates me like nothing else.

When I'm not coding, I'm a freelance nerd. I enjoy RPG video games, tabletop RPGs, fantasy novels/comics, and long walks. If you don't know that RPG stands for, just realize that I'm a nerd.
